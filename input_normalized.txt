Unsupervised Learning (The Mystery Box Challenge)

Inga Teacher-um kidayadhu, labels-um kidayadhu. Neenga mattum dhaan.

The Story: Ungalai oru room-la adachu, oru 1000 grocery items-a kotti vachitanga. Edhukum per (name) ezhudha illa. Ippo neenga enna pannuveenga? Answers kuduka maatom. Machine adhuva Patterns-a kandupudikum.

Key Term: Clustering (Kootama pirikiradhu).

Usage: Customer Segmentation (Yaar ellam Pizza virumbi sapduravanga, yaar ellam Salad group nu pirikiradhu).

Simple-a sonna: Without Answer Key, suyam-a purinjikuradhu.

Semi-Supervised Learning (The Half-Baked Knowledge)

Idhu Supervised + Unsupervised oda combo.

The Story: Unga kitta 100 dabba iruku. Adhula 5 dabba mela mattum dhaan per (Label) otti iruku ("Salt", "Sugar", "Chilly Powder","tea","coffee"). Meethi 95 dabba blank-a iruku. Neenga enna panreenga?

Therinja 5 dabba-va vechi kathukureenga.

Adha vechi, matha dabba-va smell panni/taste panni, "Oh, idhu Salt mariye dhaan iruku, so idhuvum Salt dhaan" nu guess panreenga.

Tech Connect: Concept: Konjam Labeled Data + Neraya Unlabeled Data.

Why? Label panradhu (Data entry) romba costly and time taking. So irukura konjam data va vechi mathadha guess panradhu.

Usage: Google Photos (Oru photo-la "Idhu unga Appa" nu tag panniteenga na, matha ellam photos-layum adhuve "Idhu Appa va?" nu kekum la? Adhu dhaan).

Kozhandhai oru sweet-a edukudhu - greater than Inippa iruku (Reward). - greater than Baby learns: Sweet is Good.

Adhuve thappu panni, adi vaangi, thirumba sari panradhu.

Role of Mathematics in Machine Learning (Alavu thappuna...?)

Oru chinna example. Neenga oru Tea podreenga.

Algorithm: Tea podum murai.

Data: Paal, Tea thool, Sugar.

Maths: "Oru cup paalu-ku, arai spoon sugar podu." Indha Ratio (Vigidham) dhaan Maths.

Difference Between Descriptive and Inferential Statistics Idhu rendu vagaiyana analysis methods.

Descriptive Statistics (The Menu Card / Report Card)

Idhu "Nadantha vishayatha" apdiye sollum.

Scenario: Unga hotel-la ponna vaaram evlo sales aachu?

Data: "Last week 500 Dosa vithuchu. Average-a oru aalu 2 Dosa saptanga." Tech: Mean (Average), Median (Nadula irukuradhu), Mode (Adhigama vithadhu).

Summary: Summarizing the Past.

Inferential Statistics (The Prediction)

Idhu "Nadaka poradaha" guess pannum.

Scenario: Ponna vaaram 500 Dosa vithuchu... So, adutha vaaramum 500 Dosa vikkum nu arisi-a stock panradhu.

Concept: Oru spoon Sambar-a taste panni paathutu (Sample), "Motham Sambar-um super-a iruku" (Population) nu solreenga la? Adhu dhaan Inferential Stats.

Summary: Predicting the Future based on a Sample.

Probability vs Statistics in ML (Coin Toss vs Data Analysis)

Rendum onnu kidayadhu AI Minds.

Statistics (The Detective): Unga kitta oru empty Biryani dabba iruku. Adhula irukura masala smell, otti irukura rice ellam vechi, "Idhu Hyderabadi Biryani ah irukanum" nu kandupudikuradhu.

Data iruku - greater than Karanatha kandupudikuradhu.

Probability (The Gambler): Unga kitta arisi, masala ellam iruku. "Idha vechi samacha, Biryani nalla varadhukku 80% vaipu (chance) iruku" nu solradhu.

Process theriyum - greater than Result-a guess panradhu.

ML Context: Namma Statistics use panni Model-a train pannuvom. Andha Model Probability use panni result-a predict pannum ("80% chance this email is Spam").

How ML Models Learn from Data (The Feedback Loop)

Oru Machine epdi dhaan kathukudhu?

Step 1: The Guess (Prediction) Cook (Machine) first time uppu podrom.

(Random Value).

Guess: "2 Spoon uppu podren." Step 2: The Taste Test (Loss Function) Head Chef (Actual Data) vandhu taste panraru.

Reality: "Romba uppa iruku! 1 spoon dhaan potrukanum." Error (Loss): Prediction (2) - Actual (1) = 1 Spoon Error.

Step 3: The Adjustment (Optimizer / Gradient Descent)

"Okay, next time uppa kuraikanum." Cook adutha vaati 1.5 spoon podraru. Error kuraiyum. Aduthu 1.2 spoon. Kadaisila, Error = 0 aagura varaikum adjust pannite iruparu.

Indha process-ku per dhaan "Learning". (Mathematically: Minimizing the Loss Function).

Bias, Variance, and Uncertainty (The Three Villains)

Oru nalla Cook-ku indha moonu vishayam theriyanum.

Bias (The Stubborn Cook - Underfitting)

Ivaru romba "Morattu Chef". Yaaru enna sonnalum kekka maataru.

Behavior: Avaruku "Kaaram" pidikadhu. So, Customer "Spicy Chicken" ketta kooda, avaru sweet-a dhaan seivaru.

ML: Model romba simple-a iruku. Data-va ozhunga padikama, "Ellam onnu dhaan" nu thappa predict pannum.

Result: High Error. (Customer gaandu aagiduvan).

Variance (The Over-Sensitive Cook - Overfitting)

Ivaru romba "Bayandha Chef". Ovvoru customer-kum maathi maathi seivaru.

Behavior: Oru customer "Uppu pathala" nu sonna, adutha customer-ku uppu mootaya kottuvaru. Innoruthar "Uppu adhigam" nu sonna, uppe illama seivaru.

ML: Model training data-va apdiye "Manapadam" pannidum. Pudhu data (New Customer) vandha, adhuku enna panradhu nu theriyadhu.

Result: Training-la super marks, aana Real world-la fail.

Uncertainty (The Bad Ingredient)

Scenario: Neenga correct-a dhaan samacheenga. Aana ulla potta "Muttai" azhugi poyi irundha? Adhu unga thappu illa.

ML: Datanegative noise-a (thappa) irundha, prediction-um thappa dhaan irukum. "Garbage In, Garbage Out".

The Golden Balance (Bias-Variance Tradeoff): Oru nalla ML Model (Chef)

epdi irukanum na: Basic recipes-a strong-a therinju vechikanum (Low Bias).

Chinna chinna customer feedbacks-ku romba over-a react panna koodadhu (Low Variance).

Chef's Note (Summary): Maths = Ratio and Recipe consistency.

Statistics = Analyzing past taste to improve future taste.

Learning = Taste paathu, error-a kuraikiradhu.

Stubborn-a iruka koodadhu (Bias), Over-reactive aavum iruka koodadhu (Variance).

Data-va rendaa pirikalam. Epdi "Solid Food" vs "Liquid Food" nu iruko, andha maari.

Numerical Data (Numbers - Enngal)

Idha count panna mudiyum, alakka mudiyum.

Discrete (Muzhu Number): "Ethana Muttai (Eggs) iruku?" Neenga 2 muttai, 3 muttai nu dhaan solla mudiyum. 2.5 muttai solla mudiyadhu.

Continuous (Alavu): "Evlo Paal (Milk) iruku?" Idhu 1.5 Litre, 1.75 Litre nu decimals-la varum.

Categorical Data (Vagaigal - Labels)

Idhu numbers illa, categories.

Nominal (Just Peyar): "Veg-a? Non-Veg-a?", "Apple-a? Orange-a?". Inga onnu perusu, onnu sirusu nu illa. Just names.

Ordinal (Order mukkiyam): "Customer Rating".

1 Star less than 3 Star less than 5 Star. Inga oru Order (Varisai) iruku.

Population vs Sample (Soru vs Oru Soru)

Idhu romba famous ana example.

Population (The Whole Pot): Unga veetla oru periya satti niraya Sambar seireenga. Andha Sambar mothamum serthu dhaan Population.

Sample (The Tasting Spoon): Uppu sariya iruka nu paaka, neenga motha Sambar-ayum kudika maateenga. Oru chinna spoon-la eduthu taste panreenga la? Adhu dhaan Sample.

ML Logic: Namma kitta ulagathula irukura ella data-vum (Population)

kidaikadhu. So, kedaikura konjam data (Sample) va vechi dhaan namma train pannuvom. Sample nalla irundha, Population-um nalla iruku nu artham.

Measures of Central Tendency (Nadula Enna Iruku?)

Oru 100 customer unga hotel-la sapduranga. Avanga ovvorutharum ovvoru amount-ku bill pay panranga. "Average-a" evlo collection aachu? nu therinjika 3 ways iruku.

Mean (Average - Saraasari): Calculation: Ella bill amount-ayum kooti, 100 aala divide panradhu.

Problem: Oru Panakaaran vandhu 1 Crore-ku saapta, Average romba adhigama kaatum. (Sensitive to outliers).

Median (The Middle Guy - Naduvan): Calculation: 100 bill-ayum chinna amount-lerndhu periya amount varaikum line-a adukunga. Correct-a 50-vadhu aalu evlo pay pannaro, adhu dhaan Median.

Benefit: Ambani vandhalum, Median perusa maaradhu. Safe bet.

Mode (The Bestseller - Adhigam): Calculation: Endha amount adhigama repeat aagudhu? Most people 100 roobaiku saaptu irundha, Mode = 100.

Food Analogy: Unga kadaiyila adhigama vikkura dish (Eg: Idly).

Measures of Dispersion (Consistency Check)

"Saraasari" (Average) mattum therinja podhadhu. Taste evlo Maarum (Vary)

nu theriyanum.

Range: Lowest Price (10 Rs) vs Highest Price (1000 Rs). Range = 990.

Variance and Standard Deviation (SD): Idhu romba mukkiyam. Unga samayal "Consistency" pathi pesudhu.

Low SD: Entha McDonald's la pogi saptalum Burger orey taste-la irukum.

(Data points average kitta irukum).

High SD: Namma ooru Kaiyendhi Bhavan maari. Oru naal super-a irukum, oru naal kevalama irukum. (Data points romba spread aagi irukum).

ML Logic: Model-ku Low Standard Deviation irundha dhaan nalladhu.

Prediction consistent-a irukum.

Probability Theory Basics (Vaaipu Illa Raja?)

Samayal-la edhuvum 100% guarantee kidayadhu.

"Indha maavu-la Cake nalla varuma?" - greater than "90% varum, 10% karuga vaaipu iruku." Idhu dhaan Probability. Chance of an event happening.

P(Success) + P(Failure) = 1. (Motha vaaipu eppovum 100% or 1).

Probability Distributions and The Normal Distribution The Buffet Story: Oru kalyana veettu Buffet-a imagine pannunga.

Nadu pakuthi (The Hump): Neraya peru (say 70%) "Normal" alavu saapduvanga. (Average eaters).

Left Side Tail: Romba kammiya peru dhaan "Diet" nu solli oru Idly sapduvanga.

Right Side Tail: Romba kammiya peru dhaan "Bakasuran" maari 20 Idly sapduvanga Indha shape-ku per dhaan Normal Distribution.

Why Important? Ulagathula neraya vishayam (Height, Weight, Shoe Size, Test Marks) indha Bell Curve pattern-la dhaan irukum. Namma ML Models, data indha shape-la irukum nu nambi dhaan velai seiyum.

Correlation vs Causation (Thodarbu vs Karanam)

Idha purinjikalana, neenga thappa mudivu eduthuruveenga.

Correlation (Thodarbu): Rendu vishayam onna nadakum.

Example: Veyil kalam (Summer) vandha, Ice Cream sales-um adhigam aagum.

So, Summer increase aana, Ice Cream sales increase aagum. Idhu Correlation.

Causation (Karanam): Onnu nadantha dhaan innonu nadakum.

Question: Neenga Ice cream sapdradha niruthuna, veyil adikuradhu ninnuduma? Illa!

Reality: Sun (Veyil) dhaan Karanam (Cause). Adhu dhaan Ice cream sales increase pannuchi.

Warning: Just because A and B move together, doesn't mean A causes B.

(Ice cream sapta kannadi vikkum nu namba koodadhu).

Covariance and Correlation (Direction and Strength)

Covariance: Rendu vishayam orey direction-la pogudha?

Arisi (Rice) adhigama potta - greater than Thanni (Water) adhigama oothanum. (+ Positive Covariance).

Season-la Mambalam sales adhigam achi na - greater than Matha fruits sales kuraiyum. (- Negative Covariance).

Correlation Coefficient (r): Idhu -1 to +1 kula irukum.

+1: Perfect Friends (Rice and Water).

-1: Enemies (Price and Demand).

0: Sambandhame illa (Unga shoe size-kum, unga salary-kum relation illa).

Outliers (Kalla Mannu)

Neenga rasam saadham sapdum bodhu, nadula oru "Kallu" (Stone) kadipatta epdi irukum? Mood-e poidum la? Adhu dhaan Outlier.

Definition: Matha data points kooda otti varama, thaniya nikkura data.

Example: Customer Ages: 20, 25, 22, 24, 105.

Indha "105" dhaan outlier.

Impact: Neenga "Average Age" paatha, indha 105 nala motha average-um thappa kaatum.

Solution: Samayalla kallu vandha thooki podra maari, ML-la outliers-a remove panniduvom (or adjust pannuvom).

Chef's Summary: Data types theriyama recipe ezhudha mudiyadhu.

Sample taste panni Population-a judge panrom.

Standard Deviation kammiya irundha dhaan taste consistent-a irukum.

Correlation vera, Causation vera (Don't confuse Ice cream with Sunglasses).

Outliers (Kallu) irundha model (pallu) udanjidum.

Handling Missing Values (Gaali Dabba)

The Story: Neenga oru Cake seireenga. Recipe-la "Sugar" podanum nu iruku. Aana Sugar dabba kaliya (Empty/Null) iruku. Ippo enna pannuveenga?

Problem: Unga kitta irukradhe kammi data na, idhu set aagadhu.

Imputation - Mean/Median (Pakkathu veetla kelu): "Ellarum sadharanam-a 2 spoon sugar poduvanga. So nanum Average-a 2 spoon potukuren." Tech: Fill missing values with the Mean (Average) of that column.

Imputation - Constant (Zero podu): "Sugar illana paravala, sugar-e podama sei." Tech: Fill with 0 or "Unknown".

What is a Feature? (Ingredient)

Extraction = Combining items to make something better.

Parametric vs Non-Parametric Models (Tiffin Box vs Buffet)

Parametric Models ( The Tiffin Box - Fixed Size)

Inga fixed rules kidayadhu. Data vara vara, model-um valarum.

Cooking Story: Buffet saapadu maari. Aalu vara vara, ilai (leaf) virinjite pogum. Slow aagum, aana romba complex ana data-va purinjikum.

Q3: "Gravy ah Dry ah?" - greater than Dry.

Result: "Okay, Chicken 65 kondu varen!" Logic: Data-va chinna chinna branch-a pirichu, kadaisiya mudivu edukuradhu.

Use Case: Loan Approval (Salary ok? Yes - greater than CIBIL ok? Yes - greater than Loan Approve).

Learning Parameters from Data (Uppu, Puli, Kaaram)

Machine Learning-la "Parameters" nguradhu dhaan namma ingredients-oda Alavu (Quantity).

x (Input): Ingredients (Thakkali).

w (Weight): Importance.

Loss Functions (The Taste Test / Kuttai)

The Story: Actual Taste (y): 10/10 (Super).

Machine Cooked Taste (\hat{y}): 6/10 (Mokkai).

Loss (Error): 10 - 6 = 4.

Machine-oda aim enna na, indha Loss-a Zero aakanum.

Types of Loss Functions: MSE (Mean Squared Error): Thappa (Error) Square panni kooturadhu.

(Regression-ku use aagum).

Cross-Entropy Loss: "Yes/No" thappa irundha use aagum.

(Classification-ku).

Optimization Concepts (Taste Adjustment)

Loss (Thappu) adhigama iruku nu therinjiduchu. Ippo adha epdi kuraikiradhu?

Inga dhaan Gradient Descent varum.

The Blind Cook Story: Imagine pannunga, neenga kannai katikitu malai mela (High Error)

irukeenga. Ungaluku pallathaakku (Low Error/Perfect Taste) nokki poganum.

Gradient (Slope): Kaala vechi paakureenga. "Inga sariva iruku, so inga eranganum." Learning Rate (Step Size): Big Steps: Rendu adi-la keezha poyidalam. Aana... pallathaaka thaandi vizha vaaipu iruku. (Overshooting).

Small Steps: Romba safe. Aana, keezha poga 10 varsham aagum. (Too slow).

Optimal Step: Correct-ana vegathula eranganum.

Summary: Optimization = Adjusting Weights (w) to minimize Loss.

Overfitting and Underfitting (Recap and Deep Dive)

Model train aagum bodhu rendu prachanai varalam.

Underfitting (Pacha Thannee): Kitchen: Arisi-a thanni-la pottu udane eduthuteenga. Vegave illa.

Tech: Model romba simple-a iruku. Pattern-a kathukave illa.

Result: High Training Error + High Test Error.

Overfitting (Karugipochu): Kitchen: Romba neram aduppula vechu, karuga vechiteenga. Satti-oda shape-ke dish maariduchu.

Tech: Model training data-va Manapaadam panniduchu. Pudhu data vandha work aagadhu.

Result: Low Training Error + High Test Error.

Train vs Test Performance (Veettu Practice vs Exam)

Idhu munnaadiye pathom, aana inga numbers vechi paapom.

Training Score (Veetla): Neenga pazhaguna questions-e exam-la vandha, 100/100 vanguveenga. Adhu perumai illa.

Test Score (Public Exam): Puthusa oru kelvi (New Data) vandha, unga model epdi perform pannudhu?

Scenario: Case A: Train Score: 99% | Test Score: 50% - greater than Overfitting (Manapaadam / High Variance).

Case B: Train Score: 60% | Test Score: 55% - greater than Underfitting (Puriyala / High Bias).

Case C: Train Score: 90% | Test Score: 88% - greater than Good Model (Gethu).

Evaluation Metrics for Regression (Alavu Sariya?)

Unga output "Numbers" ah irundha (Eg: Price prediction, Sales prediction), namma indha scales use pannuvom.

Mean Absolute Error (MAE) - Simple Mistake Scenario: Neenga 5 Dosa ketta, Machine 3 Dosa kudukudhu.

Error: |5 - 3| = 2 Dosa missing.

Idhu dhaan MAE. Verum thappa kooti average paakuradhu.

Mean Squared Error (MSE) - Punish Big Mistakes Scenario: Case 1: Uppu 1 spoon-ku badhila 1.5 spoon potta (Chinna thappu).

Case 2: Uppu 1 spoon-ku badhila 10 spoon potta (Periya thappu - Dish Gaali).

MSE enna pannum na, periya thappa Square (Error^2) panni perusa kaatum. So, model bayandhu poi periya thappu panna yosikum.

R-Squared (R^2) - Performance Percentage Idhu 0 to 1 kulla mark podum.

1 (100%): Perfect Cook.

0 (0%): Waste.

Negative: "Naan samaikuradha vida, chumma irundhale nalla irukum" moment.

Evaluation Metrics for Classification (Label Sariya?)

Unga output "Category" ah irundha (Eg: Spam/Not Spam, Cancer/No Cancer), metrics maarum.

Accuracy (Sariya?)

Offline vs Online Models (Kalyana Virundhu vs Thattu Kadai)

Offline Deployment (Batch Learning - The Wedding Feast)

Online Deployment (Real-time - The Dosa Stall)

Tech: Real-time Prediction: User input kudutha udane result varanum.

Pros: Romba fresh. Current situation-ku ethamaari maarum.

Cons: Latency (Time aagalam). Server slow aana customer gaandu aavanga.

Use Case: Uber Price (Mazhai peiya start aana udane price yerum), Fraud Detection.

Poor Data Quality (Azhuguna Thakkali)

The Story: Neenga World's Best Chef-a irukkalam. Aana unga kitta kudukkura Arisi-la kallu, Thakkali-la puzhu, Paal-la thanni irundha? Endha manushanalayum nalla Sambar vekka mudiyadhu.

The Tech Problem: Noise: Data-la theva illadha kuppai (Errors).

Missing Values: Sila edathula datanegative irukkadhu (Empty cells).

Outliers: Sambandhame illadha values (Age = 500).

Solution: "Cleaning" dhaan mukkiyam. Samaikkurathukku munnadi ingredients-a wash panra maari, data-va clean pannanum.

Lack of Domain Knowledge (Biryani-la Sakkara?)

The Story: Oru "Foreigner Chef" kitta solreenga: "Thambi, South Indian Sambar vei." Avarukku recipe theriyum, aana "Pakkuvam" theriyadhu.

Avaru nenaipparu: "Oh, South Indian food spicy-a irukkum, so 10 spoon milagai poduvom." Result: Vaaila vekka mudiyadhu.

The Tech Problem: Machine-ku Data-va pathi edhuvum theriyadhu. Adhu verum numbers. Oru Domain Expert (Sambar Specialist) illama, neenga features-a select panna koodadhu.

Example: Bank loan model-la "Shoe Size" mukkiyam nu machine nenaikkalam. Aana Bank Manager-ku dhaan theriyum adhu thappu nu.

Overfitting (Manapaadam Seidha Cook)

The Story: Invaru oru "Recipe Book Adimai". Book-la "30 seconds vendhu, pinbu 2 grams uppu podu" nu irukku. Aana anniku mazhai peiyudhu, stove heat kammiya irukku. Ivaru adha yosikkama, correct-a 30 seconds-la eduthu, 2g uppu poduvaru.

Result: Saapadu vegathu (because context puriyala).

The Tech Problem: Training Data-va apdiye Manapaadam pannidum.

Pudhu situation (Test Data) vandha, work aagadhu.

Recap: High Variance.

Underfitting (Avasara Kudukkai)

The Story: "Sambar dhane? Ellathayum onna kottu, thanni oothu, 2 whistle vidu." Taste paakka maataru, vengayam vadhakkavum maataru.

Result: Pacha thanni maari irukkum.

The Tech Problem: Model romba simple-a irukku. Data-voda patterns-a purinjikkave illa.

Recap: High Bias.

Scalability Issues (Veettu Samayal vs Kalyana Samayal)

The Story: Unga veetla 4 perukku tea poda ungalukku theriyum. Aana, "Naalikku Election meeting. 50,000 perukku Tea podanum" nu sonna? Unga veettu stove pathadhu. Unga pathiram pathadhu.

The Tech Problem: Chinna data-la (Excel sheet) Model super-a work aagum.

Aana Big Data (Terabytes) varum bodhu, model "Hang" aagidum.

Solution: Vertical Scaling: Periya Stove vangu (Bigger RAM/CPU).

Horizontal Scaling: 10 chinna stove vaangu (Distributed Computing / Spark).
